# Event-card image prompts (Haldi · Vivah · Pooja)

These are ready-to-use prompts for an AI image generator (e.g. Google Gemini
image / "nano-banana", Midjourney, Leonardo, etc.). Feed the tool ONE clear,
well-lit, **front-facing couple photo** from the `ENGEGAMENT EDIT` folder as
the character reference, then use the matching prompt below.

**Save the results into `website/images/` as `haldi.jpg`, `vivah.jpg`,
`pooja.jpg`** and they appear on the site automatically.

> Reference-photo tip: pick a frame where BOTH faces are clear and forward.
> A tight couple close-up (both faces sharp, good light) gives the AI the
> best identity lock. Solo portraits work less well for a two-person scene.

## 1 · Haldi  →  images/haldi.jpg

Use the uploaded engagement photo as the character reference.

Create a luxurious Indian Haldi ceremony featuring the same bride and groom. Keep their overall appearance, body proportions, hairstyle, facial structure, expressions, and skin tone consistent with the reference image.

Bride:
- Traditional yellow lehenga with floral dupatta
- Fresh floral jewelry (necklace, earrings, maang tikka, bangles)
- Turmeric (haldi) lightly applied on cheeks and hands
- Natural glowing makeup
- Smiling happily

Groom:
- Yellow embroidered kurta with white pajama
- Floral stole
- Haldi on face and hands
- Happy joyful expression

Scene: Traditional Indian Haldi ceremony, beautiful marigold flower decorations, yellow drapes, brass pots, banana leaves, rangoli, flower shower, friends and family celebrating in the background, bright morning sunlight, warm golden lighting.

Pose: Bride applying Haldi to groom's cheek, groom smiling at bride, natural candid laughter.

Style: Ultra realistic, 8K, HDR, luxury Indian wedding photography, cinematic, editorial, highly detailed fabric, natural skin texture, premium color grading.

Camera: 85mm lens, full body portrait, eye level, shallow depth of field.

Negative prompt: face distortion, identity change, extra fingers, extra people in foreground, blur, low quality, text, logo, watermark, AI artifacts.
